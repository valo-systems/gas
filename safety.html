Safe, Controlled Refills

Safe LPG Refill Rules

For everyone’s safety, please follow these rules before entering the refill area.

Customer safety rules

No smoking

No smoking anywhere near the refill area. Cigarettes must be extinguished before approaching the gate.

No open flames

No lighters, matches, or open flames are permitted near the controlled area.

No cellphone use

Please put your phone away while inside the controlled refill area. Take calls back at your vehicle.

No unauthorised entry

Restricted refill zones are off-limits to customers. Wait at the marked customer area.

Follow staff instructions

Our team is trained to handle LPG safely. Please follow their instructions at all times.

Damaged cylinders may be refused

For safety, we may refuse cylinders that are damaged, heavily rusted, leaking, or unsafe to pressurise.

On site

Controlled refill point at Midway Mews

Our refill area follows clear customer safety rules before cylinders are handled.

Cylinder requirements

  • Safe physical condition: no major dents, cracks, or impact damage.
  • No heavy rust: surface marks are fine, but severe rust can compromise cylinder safety.
  • Functional valve: the valve must seal properly and operate without sticking.
  • Suitable for refill: the cylinder type must match our refill equipment.
  • Staff decision is final: if a cylinder is unsafe, we will refuse to refill it.

Before you arrive

  • Check your cylinder size.
  • Confirm stock for large cylinders such as 19kg and 48kg.
  • Transport your cylinder upright where possible.
  • Make sure the valve is closed.
  • Do not bring leaking or badly damaged cylinders.

Why these rules matter

LPG must be handled carefully. These rules help protect customers, staff, vehicles, and the refill area.
